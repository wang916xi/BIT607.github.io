Welcome to HAUKAI

Haukai began in 2015 and is based in the Bay of Islands.
It specialises in Māori kai, the indigenous herbs in our recipes were
discovered, gathered and have been used in the preparation of Maori food
for hundreds of years. Welcome to Haukai to try the traditional foods used in Māori culture.
